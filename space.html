Sources & method. Prices, market caps and recent results are gathered from public market data (~9-10 Jun 2026); ratios, returns, moat scores and all forward figures are the author's illustrative estimates and normalizations, not consensus pulls. The prime contractors (Lockheed, Northrop, Boeing, RTX, Airbus) and component names are diversified beyond space, so company-level margins and returns reflect their whole aerospace & defense portfolios, not their space segments alone. Many new-space names are pre-profit: trailing P/E is n/m and forward multiples assume the modeled ramp. Airbus and SES show price in EUR; all market caps are normalized to USD billions. Reverse-DCF uses a transparent 10-year FCF-to-equity model and is least reliable for the pre-profit names.

Figures are illustrative - verify live before acting. This is an analytical tool, not investment advice; it reflects no view on your objectives, and the author is not a financial adviser.
