Sources & method. Prices, market caps, and recent fiscal-year results gathered from public market data (~9–10 Jun 2026); ratios, ROIC, FCF quality, moat scores, and all forward figures are the author's illustrative estimates and normalizations, not consensus pulls. Japanese fiscal years generally end in March; the global names (ASML, TSMC, AMAT, LRCX, KLAC) report in USD on their own calendars. SBC is immaterial for the Japanese issuers but is more meaningful for the US names, so the SBC-adjusted basis moves their margins modestly. Reverse-DCF uses a transparent 10-year explicit FCF-to-equity model with a Gordon terminal value; per-share revenue, net cash, and starting FCF margins are approximations.
