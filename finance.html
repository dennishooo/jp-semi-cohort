Sources & method. Prices, market caps and recent results are gathered from public market data (~9-10 Jun 2026); ratios, returns, moat scores and all forward figures are the author's illustrative estimates and normalizations, not consensus pulls. For banks, insurers, brokers and custodians, several metrics (EV/EBITDA, ROIC, FCF yield/margin/conversion, net debt/EBITDA) are not standard for balance-sheet businesses and are shown as rough proxies - ROE, price-to-book and earnings growth are the meaningful gauges there. European/UK names (LSEG, HSBC, Allianz) show price in native currency; all market caps are normalized to USD billions.

Figures are illustrative - verify live before acting. This is an analytical tool, not investment advice; it reflects no view on your objectives, and the author is not a financial adviser.
